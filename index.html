SUPERB YUMMY FOOD (SYF) RESTAURANT
THE FOOD THAT ARE AVAILABLE IN THIS RESTAURANT ARE:  

UDON

Udon is a type of thick wheat flour noodle that is common used in Japanese cuisine. Besides, It is adding with some ingredient such as scallions, tempura, prawn and more.

CHICKEN SALAD

Chicken salad is a salad that are adding with chicken as a main ingredient. Other common ingredients such as mayonnaise, hard-boiled egg, celery, onion, pepper, would be added.

MOCHI

Mochi is Japanese rice cake made of a short-grain japonica glutinous rice, called “mochigome”, and add some ingredients such as water, sugar, and cornstarch. Then, it will pound into paste and molded into desired shape

ODEN

Oden is a type of Japanese one-pot dishes (nabemono), and it consist several ingredients such as boiled eggs, daikon, konjac, and processed fishcakes.

RAMEN

Ramen is a Japanese dish with a translation of "pulled noodles" and it is originating in northwest China. It served in a meat or fish-based broth and often flavored with soy sauce or miso.
